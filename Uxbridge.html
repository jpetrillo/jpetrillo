    A small town in rural suburban Massachusetts, Uxbridge is a historic mill and textile giant. The icon incorporates a monogram of a ‘U’ and ‘X’ to portray the motion of a shuttle going through a loom. This system looks to the past for inspiration, and abstracts the idea of weaving and textiles; however, it aims at looking towards a brighter future.

    Taking influence from the physical space and surroundings of the town, this system aims to celebrate the beautiful natural surroundings and colonial architecture of the village, through a blend of color, juxtaposition of modern and classic typography, and a contemporary and geometric take on textiles and weaving.
